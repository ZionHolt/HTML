Triple Gooberberry Sunrise

bingingwithbabish (original recipe)

This recipe is called the Triple Gooberberry Sunrise, and is inspired by the frosty treat served to Spongebob and Patrick in, "The Spongebob Squarepants Movie". The recipe caontains a considerable amount of ingredients, but is easy to follow and is explained in detail.

Ingredients

Custard Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 15 g non diastatic malt powder
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 15 g carrageenan
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 1 vanilla bean pod, sliced and scraped
  • Ice
  • Salt
  • Water

Assembly Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, peeled and halved
  • 3 cherries
  • 3 M&Ms
  • 1 licorice string
  • Chocolate fudge
